About Us

Noolezhai is a quality and customer satisfaction oriented company with a global vision. Over the past 23 years, we reached a pre-eminent position in manufacturing sweaters with fully operating facilities in Tirupur.

We are the only manufacturer of sweaters in Tirupur with an installed capacity of 50 knitting machines along with state-of-the-art fully computerised STOLL brand machines from Germany.

Mission & Vision

Mission

With a total annual production capacity of 2,000,000 pieces, we make it easier to produce flat knits products. We help clients find new items and materials to guarantee prompt delivery. Goods are procured with consideration for novelty in designs, quality and timeless elegance.

Vision

Our vision is a never-ending pursuit of perfection from infants to elderly people, using materials like cotton, wool, viscose, modal, angora, acrylic, organic merino wool, wool silk and all kinds of blended yarn. We also manufacture accessories such as caps, shawls, scarves and throws.

Clients

Our international clientele is based in Europe, Canada, USA, Far East and Australia.

Quality

Our quality-conscious team adheres to the best clean room practices and quality compliance to ensure the best sustainability and indisputable quality of our products.

  • Skilled workforce
